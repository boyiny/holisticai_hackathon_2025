------------------------------

SCIENTIFIC STANDARDS
====================

EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS
---------------------
All interventions must meet ONE of the following criteria:
1. Level 1 evidence from randomized controlled trials
2. Strong observational data from large cohorts
3. Mechanistic evidence + Phase 2 clinical trials
4. Accepted medical practice for related indications

We do NOT offer:
- Stem cell therapies (except FDA-approved)
- Young blood transfusions
- Unproven peptides
- Experimental drugs without IRB approval

RESEARCH PARTNERSHIPS
---------------------

- Clients may be offered participation in IRB-approved studies

CONTINUOUS MONITORING

- Regular safety monitoring for all interventions
