Company Services and Policies

Services:
- baseline_bloodwork: includes CBC, lipid panel, fasting glucose. Contraindications: none. Price: 120
- vo2_test: treadmill-based assessment. Contraindications: unstable cardiovascular conditions. Price: 150
- scan: whole-body low-dose imaging. Contraindications: pregnancy. Price: 300
- lifestyle_coaching: 30-min session. Contraindications: none. Price: 80

Eligibility Rules:
- All tests require informed consent. Medical clearance required for VO2 if cardiovascular history present.
- Scans limited to max once per 12 months.

Scheduling Policies:
- Appointments are 1 hour slots at the clinic. Staff roles: lab tech, nurse, coach.
- Cancellations require 24 hours notice.

Disclaimers:
- Services are educational and not medical treatment. Consult licensed clinicians for diagnosis or therapy.
